ISRS Governance

Leadership and Organizational Structure

The Innovative Science Research Society is governed by a dedicated Board of Directors and an esteemed advisory council. Our leadership is committed to transparency, ethical research practices, and the continuous advancement of scientific discovery.

Registered under the H.P. Societies Registration Act 2006 (Regd. No. 758/2013), ISRS operates as a premier Non-Government Organization dedicated to R&D.

Board of Directors

Elected by the general membership, the Board oversees the strategic direction and financial health of the Society.

Advisory Council

Comprising industry veterans and academic leaders who provide guidance on long-term initiatives and partnerships.

Executive Committees

Specialized groups focused on Publications, Awards, Symposia Planning, and Public Outreach.
